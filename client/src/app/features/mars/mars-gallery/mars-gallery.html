Perseverance is NASA's car-sized rover, which landed in Mars's Jezero Crater in February 2021. It hunts for signs of ancient microbial life and is caching rock samples for a future return to Earth.

These are raw, unprocessed frames straight from the rover's cameras — often only hours old.
